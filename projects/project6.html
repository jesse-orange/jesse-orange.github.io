Mixed Chess Game Development

Design Motivation

Out of love for Xiangqi (Chinese Chess, 中国象棋 in Chinese) and Chess, I hope to design a board game that combines the two into one, allowing players to enjoy both Xiangqi and Chess.

Development Tools

  • Programming Language: python
  • Game Framework: pygame
