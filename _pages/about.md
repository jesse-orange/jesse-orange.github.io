Hi, welcome to my website. I am Jixi Gu and you also can call me Jesse.

My first name in Chinese is 缉熙 ( Jixi ).  My mother, who has a doctor of letters degree in ancient Chinese literature, told me that  “缉熙” comes from the “Da ya” (“Major Court Hymns” or “Greater Odes of the Kingdom”) section of [Shi Jing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Classic_of_Poetry) (Classic of Poetry), which collects poems or songs of praise for [Western Zhou Dynasty](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Western_Zhou) (1046-771 BC), and this word means “bright” in ancient Chinese language and in Shi Jing  is used to glorify [King Wen of Zhou](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/King_Wen_of_Zhou), the founder of Western Zhou Dynasty and first epic hero of Chinese history.

My nickname is orange. When I asked my father, who obtained a doctor of engineering degree, why they gave me this name, he smiled mysteriously and said “Because I like to eat oranges”.
